What We Offer

I.T. Support

Service Level Agreement (SLA)

All support is provided under an SLA contract where you define the services, conditions, and frequency. Work is invoiced weekly at a negotiated hourly rate with payment due within 7 calendar days.

I.T. Support

Asset Management

Serialization and control of all company technology assets and software licensing. We inventory devices, track usage, and enforce exit procedures to maintain asset accountability when employees leave.

I.T. Support

User Administration

Beyond creating user accounts, we train employees on basic security, implement best practices, and integrate with HR procedures to keep your data and network safe from unauthorized access.

I.T. Support

Help Desk & Virus Protection

Onsite desk-side support with a full ticketing system for complete visibility into services rendered. We manage anti-virus programs with cloud-based protection for email, web, identity theft, and real-time threat notifications.

I.T. Support

Remote Assistance

When onsite visits are not needed or possible, we offer remote assistance via secure connection, or phone support for step-by-step guidance when remote connectivity is unavailable.

I.T. Support

Cloud Applications

User training and application support for all mission-critical software — whether installed on your local server or hosted in the cloud — including full deployment assistance and ongoing support.
