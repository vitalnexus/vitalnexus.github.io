California Residents

Privacy Information Specific to California Residents

Source: https://oag.ca.gov/privacy/ccpa. The California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018 (CCPA) gives visitors to this Site control over the personal information that businesses collect about them. These rights include:

  • The right to know about the personal information a business collects about them and how it is used and shared
  • The right to delete personal information collected from them (with some exceptions)
  • The right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of their personal information
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising their CCPA rights

Additionally, Proposition 24, recognized as the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) was approved by votes on November 3, 2020, took effect on December 16, 2020, and the operation of the act became active on January 1, 2023. Enforcement will take place on or after July 1, 2023.

How Is a 'Business' Defined by the CCPA / CPRA?

The CCPA, as amended by the CPRA, defines a "business" as a for-profit legal entity that collects consumers' personal information, determines the purposes and means of the processing, "does business" in California, and satisfies at least one of the following thresholds:

  • Has annual gross revenues in excess of $25 million
  • Annually buys, receives, sells, or shares the personal information of 100,000 or more consumers or households
  • Derives 50% or more of its annual revenues from selling consumers' personal information

This Site, based on the definitions in the CPRA and the CCPA, does not meet the requirements of a business that is required to comply with CPRA/CCPA, but acknowledges the importance of consumer privacy and therefore provides this compliance information.

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

The right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information as defined in the CCPA is not applicable for this website. However, this Site will make every effort to comply with your requests concerning the private data collected through this Site.
